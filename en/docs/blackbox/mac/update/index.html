Update and Delete

To update 1Album Blackbox, you must delete the existing one then reinstall. As long as the /data volume links to the same local folder, the content of the blackbox will remain the same, and it does not need to be added to your account again either.

To delete the blackbox, click the X mark as circled below. You can also go to the Settings -> General page and click the DELETE CONTAINER button.

After it is deleted, you can follow the installation guide to reinstall the blackbox.
