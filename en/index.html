How does it work

Step 1: Install 1Album Blackbox


1Album Blackbox is the storage backend for 1Album. It can be installed on Windows, Mac OSX, Linux, NAS devices, or any environment that can run a Docker container.

Step 2: Sign in to 1Album app


Install 1Album app on your iPhone or iPad and sign in with your Facebook account. Other sign in methods are comming.

Step 3: Link 1Album Blackbox to your account


Link 1Album Blackbox to your account by entering your blackbox’s device code in 1Album app.

That's it!


Now 1Album app will back up photos and videos on your iPhone and iPad to your 1Album Blackbox automatically. You can delete the backed up items from your phone to free up space.

You have access to all photos and videos from your iPhone or iPad. They are served directly from the blackbox through a peer-to-peer connection.

Faster, cheaper, and more private than cloud-based alternatives.

Faster

Backing up photos is very fast with our peer-to-peer connections link directly from your phone to your computer. It is especially so when they are in the same local network.

Cheaper

Storing any amount of full size photos and videos with 1Album is absolutely free, as opposed to most cloud services which charge you by the amount of data you have.

Our business is supported by displaying limited amount of Ads in 1Album app. You can pay a small monthly fee to make the Ads go away, but this is completely optional.

More Private

1Album app and 1Album Blackbox communicate through encrypted peer-to-peer channels without relaying over cloud services or strangers’ computers.

With 1Album, your photos and videos are stored only on devices that you own. They will never be sent to anyone else.

Just the way you want it.

Full Size Backup

1Album app backs up full size photos and videos to 1Album Blackbox, so you won’t loose any details in your photos. Live photos are also supported. If a photo have been edited on iPhone, both the edited and the original versions are backed up.

Easy to Setup

1Album app and 1Album Blackbox do not need special network settings to connect. Our UDP-based peer-to-peer connection can pass through most NAT routers and cellular networks.

If your computer can browse the web, 1Album app can connect to 1Album Blackbox on that computer. It’s that easy.

Fail Safe

You can link multiple 1Album Blackboxes to your account, and they synchronize with each other automatically. You can put them in different pyhysical locations to be even safer. You will not loose your photos should any one of them fail.

Not connected? No problem.

Works Offline

1Album app works offline as well as online. Short from viewing photos that have not been cached, you can almost do everything offline, such as organizing photos or posting comments. The changes will synchronize back to 1Album Blackbox once you are online.

As a result, you can install 1Album Blackbox on a laptop and turn the blackbox on only when needed. It does not need to 24x7.be running

Works without Blackbox

If you don’t have any blackboxes or can’t connect to one, 1Album app can still sychronize photos and videos between iPhone and iPad. You can view photos on your iPhone from an iPad and vise versa. It even works with more than two devices.

All these are performed via peer-to-peer connections without sending your photos to the cloud. Just like magic.
